My Money App Policy

Data Collection

We collect data necessary to provide our services, including account registration information, financial data, device information, and user behavior data.

Data Use

We use collected data to provide and improve our services, personalize user experience, detect and prevent fraud, comply with legal obligations, analyze user behavior to improve features, and send newsletters and marketing materials with user consent.

Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties, except with user consent, to comply with legal obligations, to protect our rights or property, with third-party service providers, or in the event of a merger or acquisition.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect user data, including encryption, secure data storage, regular security audits, two-factor authentication, and data backups.

User Rights

Users have the right to access their data, correct or delete their data, object to data processing, request data portability, and opt-out of marketing communications.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance user experience and track usage. Users can manage cookie preferences in-app.

Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes will be communicated to users through the app or email.
